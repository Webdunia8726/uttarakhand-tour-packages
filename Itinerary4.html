Day 1: Arrival & Welcome

  • Arrival at Nainital: Check-in to your hotel.
  • Evening Leisure: Explore the local surroundings or relax.
  • Overnight Stay: Enjoy a peaceful night at the hotel.

Day 2: Nainital Sightseeing

  • Visit Kainchi Dham: Known for its peaceful environment and temple.
  • Explore Naina Devi Shakti Peeth: A sacred pilgrimage spot.
  • Boat Ride on Naini Lake: Enjoy a scenic ride.
  • Stroll along Mall Road: Shop and explore local attractions.
  • Visit Hanuman Garhi: Panoramic views and spiritual ambiance.
  • Return to the hotel and relax.

Day 3: Bhimtal, Nakuchiyatal & Ghorakhal

  • Visit Bhimtal: A beautiful lake for boating and scenic views.
  • Explore Nakuchiyatal: A serene lake surrounded by lush greenery.
  • Visit Sattal & Garud Tal: Still raw and peaceful.
  • Ghorakhal Goljyu Temple: Known for its peaceful atmosphere and scenic views.
  • Return to the hotel and relax.

Day 4: Final Visit & Departure

  • Enjoy Breakfast at the Hotel: A relaxing start to the day.
  • Visit Hanuman Mandir Bhumiyadhar: A spiritual visit before departure.
  • Depart to Delhi.
