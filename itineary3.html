2 Nights, 3 Days Unexplored Trip

Itinerary (Kathgodam to Kathgodam)

Contact Us Book Your Trip

Day 1: Kathgodam to Bhimtal, Pari Tal Trek

  • 8:00 AM: Pick-up from Kathgodam Railway Station by car.
  • 10:00 AM: Arrive at Bhimtal, check-in to the property.
  • 11:00 AM: Meet the guide and begin the Pari Tal Trek (2 km by riverside).
  • 12:30 PM: Reach Pari Tal Lake. Relax and enjoy lunch by the lakeside.
  • 2:00 PM: Return to Bhimtal property.
  • 4:00 PM: Free time to explore Bhimtal or relax.
  • 7:00 PM: Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 2: Karkotaka Mandir Trek

  • 7:30 AM: Breakfast at the property.
  • 9:00 AM: Meet the guide and start the Karkotaka Mandir Trek (3 km).
  • 11:00 AM: Reach the temple, explore surroundings, and enjoy views.
  • 1:00 PM: Trek back to Bhimtal.
  • 2:30 PM: Lunch at the property.
  • 4:00 PM: Free time or local sightseeing.
  • 7:00 PM: Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3: Bhimtal to Kathgodam

  • 7:30 AM: Breakfast at the property.
  • 9:00 AM: Check-out and depart for Kathgodam.
  • 11:00 AM: Arrive at Kathgodam Railway Station.
