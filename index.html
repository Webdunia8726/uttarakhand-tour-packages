Frequently Asked Questions

The best time to visit Uttarakhand is from March to June and September to November, offering pleasant weather for sightseeing and trekking.

Some must-visit places include Nainital, Mussoorie, Rishikesh, Haridwar, Auli, Kedarnath, and Badrinath.

You can travel by bus, taxi, or rental car. Uttarakhand also has a well-connected railway network and some domestic airports.
